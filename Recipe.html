Szechuan Pork and Baby Bok Choy

Step One

Marinade

Ingredients

  • 1 Boneless pork sirloin
  • 1 Tbl Soy Sauce
  • 1 tsp. Fish Suace
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 2 eggs

Instructions:

  1. Slice pork into thin strips
  2. Mix all ingrents, including pork, into a bowl
  3. Refrigerate overnight, or for a minumum of 8 hours
  4. Remove pork strips and fry with lots of oil, drain

Step Two

Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbl Hoisen
  • 2 Tbl Spicy Fermented Black Beans
  • 1/4 cup Soy Sauce

Instructions:

  1. Mix ingredients into a bowl
  2. That's it!

Step Three

Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • Baby Bok Choy
  • Carrots
  • Szechuan Chili
  • Scallions

Instructions:

  1. Slice vegetables to desired size
  2. Fry on high heat for 5 minutes
  3. Once cooked, combine veggies with pork and toss

Enjoy!
